## Danger Notes
- **DEEP SEWERS ARE A MOVEMENT TRAP**: The watery sewers (north/east of the Dark Passageway) drain 6+ movement per step. Went from 83 to 2 movement exploring them carelessly. Character got stuck exhausted at the Ledge, unable to move. DO NOT EXPLORE without careful rationing of movement.
- Common Square has multiple hostile-looking mobs together — risky at level 1.
- Sewers below the practice yard are a one-way trip down from the yard (no
  "u" back from the dead-end room) — go back via s→Junction→n or retrace exits.
- **Next session strategy**: Explore NORTH from Temple Square (not via sewers) to find the newbie zone with the minotaur.
